---

layout: recipe
title:  "Mafiosinudeln"
servings: 4

tags:
- Mittagessen
- Kochen

ingredients:
- 4|bananas
- 0.5|cup|butter
- 0.5|cup|sugar
- 2|eggs
- 2|cups|flour
- 0.5|tsp|baking soda
- 1|tsp|baking powder
- 1|pinch|salt
- 0.25|cup|nuts (we like pecans)

directions:
- Beat the eggs, then cream with the butter and sugar
- Mix in bananas, then flour, baking soda/powder, salt, and nuts
- Add to greased and floured pan
- Bake until brown/cracked, toothpick comes out clean
